Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • 2 teaspoons of butter

Scrambled eggs recipe

Description

A great recipe for breakfast!!!

Directions

  1. Beat the eggs, salt, and pepper in a bowl
  2. Heat the butter in a large nonstick skillet
  3. Pour in the egg mixture into the skillet
  4. Pull the eggs across the pan with a spatula, gently folding and lifting until the egg is cooked
